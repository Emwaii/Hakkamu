About Hakkamu

HAKKAMU FIRM adalah firma hukum terkemuka di Indonesia yang berspesialisasi dalam Kekayaan Intelektual ( HAKI ), Penyelesaian Sengketa, dan Pendirian & Perizinan.

Memang, kami bukan firma hukum konvensional. Kami mengkhususkan diri dalam memandu klien melalui berbagai aspek peraturan yang kompleks, mengidentifikasi dan mengurangi risiko dan kewajiban, serta memberikan nasihat strategis untuk menginformasikan pengambilan keputusan yang penting. Tujuan utama kami adalah menyelesaikan setiap urusan klien kami dengan baik dengan menata ulang berbagai kemungkinan dan menggabungkan ketajaman hukum dengan pemikiran yang inovatif.

Nasihat kami berakar pada pemahaman yang mendalam mengenai tujuan bisnis klien kami dan tantangan praktis yang mereka hadapi. Kami mengubah hambatan hukum menjadi peluang baru, menyelaraskan layanan kami dengan nama perusahaan kami: Your Legal Needs.

Tim HAKKAMU terdiri dari para profesional hukum yang bersemangat dan berdedikasi tinggi yang berkomitmen untuk melampaui harapan klien kami. Kami bercita-cita untuk menjadi lebih dari sekedar penasihat hukum tradisional; kami berusaha untuk memberikan hasil yang luar biasa dan mencapai hasil yang nyata.

HAKKAMU FIRM is a distinguished Indonesian law firm specializing in Intellectual Property (IP), Dispute Resolution, and Establishment & Licensing.

Indeed, we are not a conventional law firm. We specialize in guiding clients through complex regulatory landscapes, identifying and mitigating risks and liabilities, and providing strategic counsel to inform critical decision- making. Our ultimate goal is to successfully conclude our clients' transactions by reimagining possibilities and combining legal acumen with innovative thinking.

Our advice is rooted in a deep understanding of our clients' business objectives and the practical challenges they face. We transform legal obstacles into new opportunities, aligning our services with our firm name: Your Legal Needs.

HAKKAMU'S team is composed of passionate and dedicated legal professionals committed to exceeding our clients' expectations. We aspire to be more than traditional legal advisors; we strive to deliver exceptional results and achieve tangible outcomes.

Our Code of Work

HAKKAMU’S Code of Work outlines the standards of practice, ethical principles, and professional responsibilities that we must adhere to:

HAKKAMU and it’s Advocates maintain independence and act in the best interests of our clients.

HAKKAMU and it’s Advocates keep our client’s information confidential.

HAKKAMU and it’s Advocates maintain a high level of competence and skill in our practice.

HAKKAMU and it’s Advocates act with honesty and integrity in all their dealings.

HAKKAMU Advocates conduct ourselves professionally at all times.

Practice Area

INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

PROTECTION

HAKKAMU Firm provides comprehensive legal services in all areas of Intellectual Property (IP) protection, including copyright, industrial design, trademark, geographical indications, and patents. Our IP protection practice is distinguished by a holistic approach, rooted in years of experience and active participation in the development of the country's IP laws. We have successfully managed the IP portfolios of multinational corporations across diverse sectors, such as food and beverages, consumer goods, environmental products, retails, and pharmaceuticals.

DISPUTE AND LITIGATION

Over the course of our extensive experience in Intellectual Property Dispute and Litigation within Indonesia, we have fostered a close collaborative relationship with the national police. Our firm has provided critical support in IP enforcement matters, including participation in criminal investigations led by the Special Crime Directorate, anti-counterfeiting raids, and the delivery of IP enforcement training programs to police and other law enforcement personnel. Our diverse client base spans multiple industries, with a particular emphasis on fashion and retail

ESTABLISHMENT

With its robust economic growth and substantial population, Indonesia has emerged as a leading destination for foreign investment. However, navigating the Indonesian business landscape can present significant challenges. Our establishment services offer a comprehensive solution, guiding investors through the entire process of setting up operations across various sectors. Beyond establishment, we specialize in identifying and mitigating potential risks and liabilities to safeguard our clients' interests.

LICENSING

We believe that establishing a business in Indonesia presents significant challenges. To address this, HAKKAMU has expanded its services to assist clients in obtaining the necessary permits and licenses. Our team is dedicated to conducting thorough research, gathering all required government compliances, and fulfilling our clients' needs to facilitate the licensing process.
